About

Belo Horizonte is the name of the place where I come from. It means ‘beautiful horizon’ and it was my inspiration to start this company here in New Zealand.
With over 10 years experience back in my home country, where I used to work in farms and country side properties, garden, plants and grounds are my passion.
I really appreciate your contact and the opportunity to take care of your garden. You can trust we will do our best to make and keep your garden clean, fresh, vibrant and beautiful.
We offer great services and very competitive prices! You won’t regret to have my Belo Horizonte at your Belo Garden!
